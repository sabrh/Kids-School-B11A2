Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Focuses on learning the alphabet, numbers, shapes, colors, and basic social skills through play and fun activities.

Elementary School

Builds foundational knowledge in reading, writing, math, science, and social studies with hands-on projects and creative exploration.

Middle (10-16) Years

Expands critical thinking and problem-solving skills through advanced subjects like algebra, history, science experiments, and creative writing.
